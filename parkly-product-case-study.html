Parkly.

Space → Search → Scan → Solved

01 / The problemDead space
the city isn't full —

It's just hiding its space.

Driveways sit empty all day while their owners are at work. Private lots go quiet on weekends. Drivers circle the same block looking for a spot that's ten feet away — behind a fence, off the map, un-bookable.

02 / Unused spaceThe supply
what's already there —

Turn idle space into real spots.

Parkly lets anyone with a driveway, a lot, or a spare garage bay list it in minutes. No construction, no permits — just space that already exists, put to work.

03 / Nationwide searchThe reach
one app, every city —

Wherever you drive, a spot waits.

Same city, different city, road trip across the country — Parkly's map doesn't care. Every listed spot is searchable the moment it's live, so drivers find real, bookable parking anywhere the network reaches.

03 / Nationwide searchHow it grows
it scales with people —

No lot ever needs installing.

Every new host adds one more dot to the map — instantly searchable, no infrastructure required. That's what lets Parkly cover a city, then a state, then the country, at the pace people sign up.

First host → neighborhood → city → nation
04 / The QR alertThe trust
a knock, without a number —

Scan the code. Skip the issue.

Every Parkly spot carries a QR sticker. Blocked in? Need someone to move? Scan it, send a message — it reaches the driver instantly, with neither side ever seeing the other's phone number.

04 / The QR alertThree steps
no app required to reply —
STEP 01

Scan the sticker.

Any phone camera reads the code on the spot's Parkly sticker — no download needed to send an alert.

STEP 02

Send a message.

A short web form opens. Type the issue — "blocked me in," "need you to move" — and send.

STEP 03

Driver gets notified.

The message routes straight to the driver's phone. Both sides stay anonymous, every time.

A / The host
earns.

Lists a spot, gets paid.

A driveway that made nothing yesterday makes real money today. Parkly handles booking, payment, and the QR sticker on move-in.

B / The platform
takes a small cut.

A slice of every booking.

A simple service fee on each transaction. No listing fees, no subscriptions — Parkly only earns when a spot actually gets used.

05 / The businessThe opportunity
a market the size of a country —

Built for India, first.

More two- and four-wheelers join Indian roads every year than most countries have on them entirely — and almost none of that growth came with a parking plan. Every metro, every high-rise, every market street shares the same search for the same six feet of curb.

EVERY METRO

Mumbai to Bengaluru — the same story, block by block.

EVERY BUILDING

Housing societies and offices sit on space nobody has put to work yet.

EVERY DAY

This isn't a seasonal squeeze — it's the daily default.

06 / The visionWhat's next
every idle spot, working —

A parking spot for every car.

Not by building more concrete — by using what's already there. Every driveway, lot, and garage on the network is one less car circling the block.
